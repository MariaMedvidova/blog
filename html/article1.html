    Erasmus Student Network Slovakia (ESN Slovakia):
    • is a non-profit student organisation
    • our mission is to represent international students
    • one of the local sections is ESN Kosice

    Description of the Section

    The character of our section is rather special. We were established for the purpose of Pavol Jozef Šafárik University. Moreover, we cooperate with two other universities in the city and take care of their Erasmus students; therefore, we have to be organized quite well. Difficulties in managing the section are relieved in connecting Erasmus students thus sharing ESN spirit among them.


    History of the Section

    To trace the beginning of ESN UPJŠ Košice we do not have to go far back in time, only to 2009. Founders of the section, Norbert Ostro and Mária Juraševská, have done the most difficult job negotiating with universities and becoming salient; however, much work is still to be done in order to be fully efficient.

    Universities:

    We have 3 universities:

    1. Pavol Jozef Šafárik University in Košice
    2. The University of Veterinary Medicine and Pharmacy in Košice
    3. The Technical University of Kosice
    Logo Name Description
    Pavol Jozef Šafárik University At present the University has five faculties – Faculty of Medicine, Faculty of Science, Faculty of Law, Faculty of Public Administration and Faculty of Arts – which prepare physicians and dentists, experts in natural sciences, mathematics and informatics, teachers, lawyers, specialists for public administration and specialists for psychology, philosophy and theory of communication.
    The University of Veterinary Medicine and Pharmacy This university is the only institution in the Slovak Republic offering higher education in the veterinary field. It has been providing the veterinary education in English for 15 years.
    The Technical University Today it has nine faculties, Faculty of Heavy Engineering, Faculty of Mining, Faculty of Metallurgy, Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Faculty of Civil Engineering, Faculty of Professional Studies, Faculty of Manufacturing Technologies, Faculty of Economics, Faculty of Arts and The Faculty of Aeronautics.
